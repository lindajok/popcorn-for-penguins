Little bit about formatting

The whole website is usually divided into three:
  1. Header
  2. Main
  3. Footer
These three parts can again be divided into own sections. Sections give structure to the website. Good structure helps the search engines find relevant info on your site. They also help others (and you) to understand whats happening.
